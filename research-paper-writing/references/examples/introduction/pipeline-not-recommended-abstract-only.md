# Not Recommended: Abstract-Only Pipeline

1. Do not jump directly to the method name.
2. Do not skip the bottleneck explanation.
3. Do not hide the platform and metric context.

Terminology anchors:

1. Do not replace the bottleneck with generic claims such as `the method is inefficient`.
2. State the exact missing term that should appear, such as all-reduce cost, HBM pressure, NUMA placement, I/O contention, or weak-scaling efficiency.
